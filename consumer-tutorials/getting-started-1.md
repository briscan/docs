# Open your community on BRI wallet

1. Download the BRI wallet from the following links:

* For iOS users: [https://apps.apple.com/il/app/bri-wallet/id1491783654](https://apps.apple.com/il/app/bri-wallet/id1491783654)
* For Android users: [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.bri.bricash](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.bri.bricash)

   2. After installing the app register and open the sidebar and click on "Switch community" and click "scan the QR code"

   3. Then go to your community page on the Studio and go to the "White label wallet" page and scan the QR code
